
         
           Java program to calculate Average marks and Percentage of 5 subjects




1. Get the marks of all 5 Subjects 	
		s1,s2,s3,s4,s5

2. Get the total maximum marks of all 5 subjects
 

3. Calculate the total marks, average marks, percentage


		total marks = s1+s2+s3+s4+s5

		average = total marks / total no.of subjects
        		= (s1+s2+s3+s4+s5)/5

		percentage= (total marks/total maximum marks)*100	

4. Print the calculated results
